RADITZ'S ABILITIES

Flight
The ability to fly with the use of ki.
Ki Blast
The most basic form of energy wave, creating a projectile made of ki.
Saturday Crush
Raditz holds his hand in the air, forming a pink sphere of ki before throwing it at the opponent.
Double Sunday
Raditz charges ki in both hands, forming 2 purple ki spheres. He then brings his hands forwards, releasing the spheres as energy waves towards his target.
