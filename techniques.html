RADITZ'S ABILITIES

  1. Flight: The ability to fly with the use of ki.
  2. Ki Blast: The most basic form of energy wave, creating a projectile made of ki.
  3. Saturday Crush: Raditz holds his hand in the air, forming a pink sphere of ki before throwing it at the opponent.
  4. Double Sunday: Raditz charges ki in both hands, forming 2 purple ki spheres. He then brings his hands forwards, releasing the spheres as energy waves towards his target.
